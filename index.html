About Us

MKSSS's Cummins College of Engineering for Women, Pune MH India an Autonomous Institute affiliated to Savitribai Phule Pune University. CCEW is considered to be one of the best colleges for Engineering in the eastern states. Cummins college has been given an ‘A’ grade by NAAC in the year 2012 and then from 2017 to 2019. The undergraduate and postgraduate degrees provided by the Cummins college are affiliated to the Savitribai Phule Pune University. This is the world's one of the best college. So join Us!
